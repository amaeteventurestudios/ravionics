The Gap

THE WORLD IS
LARGELY UNWATCHED

These are not estimates. They are documented figures from the agencies responsible for the mission.

13s
Aerial drone coverage per border mile per day

CBP's small UAS teams flew 9,620 total hours across 7,000 miles of US border in fiscal year 2025. Each mile receives roughly 13 seconds of aerial drone coverage per day.

6/11
Coast Guard statutory missions achievable

The Coast Guard's own analysis confirms it has enough cutters to complete only 6 of its 11 legally mandated missions. 95,000 miles of shoreline. Persistently under-covered.

1000s
Pipeline miles inspected by exception not routine

Energy infrastructure spans continents. Inspection windows are measured in days and weeks. Failures are found after they happen, not before.

∞
Critical assets beyond persistent aerial reach

Power grids. Farmland. Mining operations. Remote industrial sites. Every one of them sits beyond the economic reach of persistent manned aerial surveillance. Until now.

2.6M
Miles of US pipeline with no persistent aerial monitoring

The US has more than 2.6 million miles of pipelines delivering natural gas and liquid petroleum products. The overwhelming majority has no continuous aerial surveillance. Failures are found after they happen, not before.

90%
Of US farmland never aerially monitored in a given year

Aerial imagery was used on only 7% of corn acres and under 10% of soybean acres according to the most recent USDA data. Over 880 million acres of US agricultural land sits without persistent overhead awareness.
